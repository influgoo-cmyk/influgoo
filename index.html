The Process

From Brief to Results
in 7 Days

A clear, fast, and transparent process — so you always know what's happening with your campaign.

Step by Step

How We Run Your Campaign

01

Discovery Call & Brief

We start with a 30-minute call to understand your product, target audience, goals, and budget. From this, we build a complete campaign brief — including platform strategy, creator profile, content format, and KPIs.

You'll walk away with
  • → A clear campaign strategy doc
  • → Defined KPIs and expected outcomes
  • → Platform and creator type recommendations
02

Creator Research & Vetting

We manually research and shortlist creators that match your brand. We check audience demographics, engagement rate authenticity, niche authority, past brand performance, and content quality. You get a shortlist with full analysis for each creator.

Our vetting checks
  • → Real vs bought followers analysis
  • → Audience age/location/interest fit
  • → Brand safety & content history review
03

Outreach & Agreements

We handle all communication with creators — pitch, negotiate rates, agree on deliverables, and finalise contracts. You never have to chase a DM. Once confirmed, we collect advance payments using an escrow-style process for security.

What we handle
  • → All creator communication
  • → Rate negotiation & contracts
  • → Escrow-style payment protection
04

Content Review & Go Live

Every piece of content is reviewed by us and shared with you for approval before going live. Once you're happy, we coordinate posting schedules for maximum reach and manage all creator communication during the live phase.

Quality control
  • → Content reviewed vs your brief
  • → Your approval required before posting
  • → Coordinated post scheduling
05

Performance Report & Insights

After the campaign, we deliver a detailed report — reach, impressions, engagement rate, click-through, conversions, ROAS, and lessons learned. Real data, no fluff. We also recommend the next campaign based on what worked.

Report includes
  • → Reach, impressions, engagement
  • → Conversions & ROAS
  • → Insights & next campaign suggestions

Creators & Quality
Every creator in our network goes through a manual vetting process. We check engagement rate patterns, follower growth history, audience demographics, comment quality, and brand safety. Anyone with signs of purchased engagement is rejected before they ever touch your brand — no exceptions.
We work with micro-influencers (10K–100K followers) all the way up to top-tier creators with millions of followers. For most D2C brands, micro-influencers deliver the best ROI due to higher engagement rates and more targeted audiences. We recommend the right tier based on your goals and budget.
